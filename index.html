About isopods

Isopods are a type of crustation found all over the world, the most common for english speakers being the Armadilidium vulgare.

Armadillidium Vulgare have a black or brown body and frequently white or yellow spots, pillbugs are whats known as detritivores which means the eat dead or decaying vegetable matter, such as leaves.
While roly polies live on land, they actually still have gills which means they require moisture to breathe. How to care for isopods

Isopods typically require very little care, mostly watering the terrarium every 2-3 days. Remember to give them decaying plant matter, such as leaves or carrots, to feed on when there isnt much plant matter, and also change the soil every 1-1.5 years
